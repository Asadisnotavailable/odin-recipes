Taco

Description

Fish tacos are a taste of pure textural wizardry -- the tender bite of fish, the crispy crunch of cabbage, and the kick of creamy white sauce will put a spell on you. One bite and you're hooked. Lucky for you, we've got a world of fish taco recipes to explore! Grilled fish tacos, baked fish tacos, blackened fish tacos, and fried fish tacos. We have salmon tacos, halibut tacos, shrimp tacos, cod tacos, and tilapia tacos.

Steps

  1. Prepare the Fish Fillets
  2. Dredge the Fish
  3. Coat with Cornmeal or Breadcrumbs
  4. Heat Oil for Frying
  5. Fry the Fish
  6. Prepare the Taco Sauce
  7. Assemble the Tacos
  8. Drizzle with Sauce and Garnish
  9. Serve and Enjoy
